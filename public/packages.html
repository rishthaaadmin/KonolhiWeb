FAQ

Questions business owners actually ask

Do I really need a website if I already have Instagram?
On Instagram, the algorithm decides who sees you, and DMs bury enquiries. A website is yours: it shows up on Google, answers questions automatically, and collects enquiries in a list you control. Most businesses do best using both, with Instagram feeding the website.
What if I can't write the content?
You don't have to. We interview you on WhatsApp or in person, and write everything in clear, simple language. You approve every word before launch.
How do payments work?
In stages: typically 40% to start, 40% at design approval, 20% at launch. Bank transfer to a Maldivian account. You never pay everything upfront, and you see the design before the biggest payment.
Who owns the website after it's built?
You do, domain, content, design, everything. If you ever want to move to another provider, we hand over cleanly. Care plans are optional, never a lock-in.
Can I start small and add the dashboard later?
Yes, that's the recommended path for most businesses. Start with a Starter or Growth website, and add dashboard modules once the enquiries are flowing. Everything we build is designed to grow.
