Business dashboards

Your whole business, on one screen

Not enterprise software, and not another app your staff won't open. A simple control panel with only the modules you need, quick to check each day.

Pick your modules

Every dashboard starts with a home screen showing today's numbers. Then you add modules, each one replaces a notebook, a spreadsheet, or a pile of WhatsApp messages.

Lead CRM

Every enquiry from your website, WhatsApp and Instagram in one list, with status (new, contacted, quoted, won), notes, and a "needs follow-up today" view. Replaces: scrolling DMs hoping you didn't miss anyone.

Booking Module

Calendar view of every booking with availability, deposits and automatic confirmations. Customers book from the website; you see it instantly. Replaces: the notebook and the double-booking apologies.

Project & Task Management

Jobs broken into tasks with deadlines and owners. See which projects are on track and which are stuck, without asking around. Replaces: "I thought you were doing that."

HR Lite

Staff records, leave balances, attendance and document expiry reminders (visas, permits, contracts). Replaces: the folder that only one person understands.

Petty Cash Tracker

Every rufiyaa out, logged with reason, receipt photo and who took it. Monthly totals by category. Replaces: "where did the cash go this month?"

Inventory Lite

Stock in, stock out, low-stock alerts before you run out mid-week. Simple enough for daily use, no barcode scanners required. Replaces: counting boxes at 11pm.

Customer Portal

Your clients log in to see their order status, invoices and documents, instead of messaging you for updates. Replaces: the same status question, answered forty times.

Maintenance Tickets

Guests, tenants or staff report an issue; it gets logged, assigned and tracked to resolution. Perfect for guesthouses and property managers. Replaces: repair requests lost in group chats.

Quotation & Invoice Requests

Customers request quotes from your website; you respond with professional PDF quotations and track which ones became jobs. Replaces: quotes typed in WhatsApp that vanish.

Staff Task Board

Each staff member sees today's tasks on their phone, ticks them off, and flags problems. You see completion at a glance. Replaces: morning instructions repeated by voice note.
